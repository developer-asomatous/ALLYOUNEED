Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 9, 2026

Overview

AYN ("All You Need") is a media saving tool for personal use. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect (very little) and how we use it.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect or store the URLs you download.
  • We do not collect your personal information (name, email, phone).
  • We do not track your browsing activity.
  • We do not have user accounts or registration.
  • We do not sell any data to third parties.

Data We Collect

We collect minimal, anonymous data to improve the app:

  • Crash reports — Anonymous stack traces when the app crashes, to fix bugs. No personal data is included.
  • Usage statistics — Aggregate counts like "total downloads" stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your phone.

Advertising

AYN displays ads to keep the app free. Our ad partners may collect:

  • Device advertising ID (for personalized ads)
  • Device type, OS version, screen size
  • General location (country/region level, not precise GPS)

You can opt out of personalized ads in your device's Google Settings → Ads → Opt out of personalization.

Downloads & Content

AYN is a tool that helps you save media for personal use. Downloaded files are stored locally on your device. We do not host, store, or distribute any content. Users are responsible for ensuring they have the right to save the content they download.

Data Storage

All app data (settings, download history, preferences) is stored locally on your device using encrypted storage. No data is sent to our servers except the temporary download processing (which is immediately deleted after the file is delivered to your device).

Third-Party Services

  • Ad networks — For displaying advertisements. Subject to their own privacy policies.
  • Crash reporting — Anonymous error reports to help us fix bugs.

Children's Privacy

AYN is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at privacy@allyouneed.app.
